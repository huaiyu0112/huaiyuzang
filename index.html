Ph.D. in Statistics · University of Cincinnati

I am a Senior Biostatistician with more than 10 years of experience in clinical and translational research. Currently, I serve as Project Statistician at Tigermed-BDM, where I lead statistical support for Eli Lilly's Medical Affairs Immunology launch. Prior to joining Tigermed-BDM, I spent nearly four years at the Heart Institute Research Core at Cincinnati Children's Hospital Medical Center, serving as a senior statistician for several multicenter collaborative registries, including NEPHRON and WE-ROCK. During that time, I collaborated closely with clinicians and researchers on study design, statistical analysis, and interpretation of findings, contributing to more than 65 peer-reviewed publications.
