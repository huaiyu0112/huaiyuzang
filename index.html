Huaiyu Zang

Senior Biostatistician
Tigermed-BDM Inc., Somerset, New Jersey

I am a Senior Biostatistician with more than 10 years of experience supporting clinical trials and observational research studies. Currently, I serve as a Project Statistician at Tigermed-BDM, where I lead statistical support for Eli Lilly's Immunology Medical Affairs initiatives. Prior to joining Tigermed-BDM, I spent four years at the Heart Institute Research Core at Cincinnati Children's Hospital Medical Center, serving as a senior statistician for several multicenter collaborative registries, including NEPHRON and WE-ROCK. To date, I have contributed to more than 60 peer-reviewed publications. My professional interests include clinical research methodology, biostatistics, and the application of data-driven approaches to improve healthcare outcomes.

Outside of work, I enjoy studying history and following sports. My wife, Vicky, and I have four children: Jeremy, Bella, Gracie, and Joshua.
